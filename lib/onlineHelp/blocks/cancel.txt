CANCEL - cancel a LISTEN'er by its
listener_key

Grammar:
 "CANCEL" key

Arguments:
 - key: <listener_key> a valid key

Notes:
 - before being CANCEL'd, a listener_key
   will display a unique ID for the
   LISTEN'er internal task when PRINT'd
 - after being CANCEL'd, a listener_key
   will display \174defunct\175 when PRINT'd

Aliases:
 - "CANC"
