END - finish a block

Notes:
 - END marks the end of any block
 - The "see also" section below lists
   keywords that can open blocks that
   are finished with an END statement

See also:
 - %BEGIN% %ARRAY%
 - %BEGIN% %CREMAP%
 - %IF% (followed by THEN, ELSE or ELSEIF)
 - %FOR%
 - %WHILE%
 - %LISTEN%
 - %DECLARE% %RECORD%
