BREAK - unconditional GOTO end of loop

Grammar:
 "BREAK" [ levels ]

Arguments:
 - levels: <number> of nested loops to
   BREAK out of, must be GTE 1.
   Default 1

Notes:
 - BREAK may only be used inside FOR or
   WHILE loop blocks
 - BREAK will GOTO the levels-nth END
   statement that follows that
   corresponds with FOR and WHILE loops
   and then proceed out of that loop to
   the next statement that follows END
