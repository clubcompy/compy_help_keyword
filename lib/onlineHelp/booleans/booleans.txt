             About Booleans
             \006\006\006\006\006\006\006\006\006\006\006\006\006\006

Boolean variables in the Comfy language
can have values of true or false.
Booleans can hold "yes" (true) and "no"
(false) decisions in programs.

The Comfy language has keywords whose
primary purpose is to work with booleans:

  %EQ%       %NEQ%      %LT%       %LTE%
  %GT%       %GTE%      %AND%      %OR%
  %NOT%      %TRUE%     %FALSE%

The Comfy language has symbols for
performing operations with booleans:

 =  %assign%
 !  boolean %not%
 () %parenthesis%
