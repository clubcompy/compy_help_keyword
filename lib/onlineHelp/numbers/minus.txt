- (minus) - numeric subtraction

Grammar:
 left "-" right

Arguments:
 - left: <number|boolean>
 - right: <number|boolean>

Evaluates to:
 <number> difference of right
 subtracted from left

Notes:
 - If left or right are a boolean, it
   will be converted to a number before
   the minus. true will be 1. false will
   be 0
