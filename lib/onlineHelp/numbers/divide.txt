/ (divide) - numeric division

Grammar:
 left "/" right

Arguments:
 - left: <number>
 - right: <number>

Evaluates to:
 <number> quotient of the left divided
 by right

Notes:
 - If left or right are a boolean, it
   will be converted to a number before
   the divide. true will be 1. false
   will be 0

Aliases:
 - "\247" division sign, commonly seen
   on mobile device virtual keyboards
