* (multiply) - numeric multiplication

Grammar:
 left "*" right

Arguments:
 - left: <number|boolean>
 - right: <number|boolean>

Evaluates to:
 <number> product of left multiplied by
 right

Notes:
 - If left or right are a boolean, it
   will be converted to a number before
   the multiply. true will be 1. false
   will be 0

Aliases:
 - "\215" multiplication sign, commonly
   seen on mobile device virtual
   keyboards
