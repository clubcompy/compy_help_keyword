ATANTWOR - Gives the counterclockwise
angle in radians between the positive
x-axis and the ray (0, 0) \029 (x, y)

Grammar:
 "ATANTWOR" y "," x

Arguments:
 - y: <number> y-coordinate of ray
   endpoint
 - x: <number> x-coordinate of ray
   endpoint

Evaluates to:
 <number> angle between 0 and \227 when
 y is positive, and 0 and -\227 when y is
 negative
