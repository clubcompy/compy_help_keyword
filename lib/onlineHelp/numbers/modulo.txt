%% (modulo) - numeric modulo

Grammar:
 left "%%" right

Arguments:
 - left: <number|boolean>
 - right: <number|boolean>

Evaluates to:
 <number> remainder of left modulo right

Notes:
 - If left or right are a boolean, it
   will be converted to a number before
   the modulo. true will be 1. false
   will be 0
