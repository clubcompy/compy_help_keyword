WALK - Walk in the facing direction

Grammar:
 "WALK" [ rec "," ] units

Arguments:
 - rec: <record> that can be WALK'ed.
   Default is the Turtle record
 - units: <number> of units to walk in
   rec's coordinate system (see notes)

Notes:
 - Affected rec.x and rec.y will be set
 - Records that can be WALK'ed:
   - Turtle: pixels of the VCE display
     are its units, and the origin (0,0)
     of its coordinate system is located
     in the center of the display.
     Turtle.spd controls how many
     pixels it will walk across the
     screen for every frame drawn
