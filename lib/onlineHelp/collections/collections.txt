The Comfy language supports grouping
multiple items together into a single
variable. Arrays are an ordered
collection of items.

The Comfy language has keywords whose
primary purpose is to work with
collections:

  %ARRAY%    %DIM%      %DIMS%     %INSERT%
  %REMOVE%   %LENGTH%

The Comfy language has symbols for
performing operations with collections:

 =  %assign%
 [] %subscript%
